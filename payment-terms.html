Legal Policies

Payment & Billing Terms

Last Updated: May 30, 2026

1. Billing Cycles

All subscription plans are billed on a recurring monthly cycle starting from the day of initial infrastructure deployment. Invoices will be generated and processed automatically using the payment method placed on file.

2. Implementation Fees

Certain custom configurations (such as the Enterprise Node) require a one-time onboarding and setup fee. This fee covers the cost of domain purchasing, DNS warming configurations, and initial data scraping. Setup fees are non-refundable once architectural work has commenced.

3. Cancellations & Modifications

You may cancel or downgrade your recurring tier at any time. We require a 14-day notice prior to your next billing cycle to successfully halt the renewal charge. Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing to your dedicated Growth Engineer or via your client portal.

4. Refund Policy

Because of the hard costs associated with infrastructure deployment (software licenses, SMTP setups, and verified lead data), we do not offer standard refunds on initial subscription cycles. However, our performance guarantees (where applicable) dictate that if KPI minimums are not met within 90 days, we operate free of charge until they are.

5. Overages

If your outreach volume intentionally exceeds the maximum limits of your selected tier, overages will be billed at a pro-rated amount per 1,000 additional leads scraped and contacted. You will be notified before any overage limits are triggered.
