Buyers are blind to traditional templates. "Hey [First Name], saw you work at [Company]" is an immediate indicator of a scraped automation sequence. To generate calls, systems must simulate deep human research.

Programmatic Injection

We leverage data scrapers that pull specific, recent events—like a recent Series B funding round, a new C-level hire, or a specific technology stack newly implemented on their website. These data points act as variables.

The Outcome
